E-Sports, competitive video gaming played at a professional level, has surged in popularity, captivating millions of spectators worldwide. What was once a niche hobby has evolved into a multi-billion-dollar industry, with professional gamers competing in tournaments for substantial prize pools. Games like League of Legends, Dota 2, and Fortnite have become cultural phenomena, attracting a diverse audience of fans and enthusiasts. With live events, streaming platforms, and sponsorships driving its growth, E-Sports continues to redefine the landscape of competitive gaming.
