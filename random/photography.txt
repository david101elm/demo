Photography, born from the marriage of art and technology, has undergone a fascinating evolution since its invention in the 19th century. From the cumbersome daguerreotypes to the convenience of digital cameras, photography has democratized visual expression, allowing people to capture moments in time with unprecedented clarity and immediacy. Iconic photographs have shaped our collective memory, documenting historical events, social movements, and personal milestones. In today's digital age, smartphones have made everyone a potential photographer, transforming how we perceive and interact with the world around us.
