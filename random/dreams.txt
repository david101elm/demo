Dreams have fascinated mankind for centuries, offering a window into the subconscious mind. Psychologists study the intricate workings of dreams, exploring their symbolism, patterns, and underlying meanings. From Freud's theories of wish fulfillment to modern research on REM sleep and dreaming, understanding the psychology of dreams provides valuable insights into human cognition, emotions, and mental health. Whether they are fantastical adventures or unsettling nightmares, dreams remain a mysterious realm waiting to be explored.
