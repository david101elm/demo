Our company's database is structured to efficiently manage various aspects of our operations. It comprises several tables, including Employees, Departments, Projects, Tasks, Meetings, Assets, Inventory, Sales, Feedback, and Logs. These tables store essential information such as employee details, department information, project data, task assignments, meeting records, asset inventory, sales transactions, feedback, and system logs. Each table is designed with appropriate relationships and constraints to maintain data integrity and consistency. Primary and foreign key constraints establish relationships between tables, while indexes optimize query performance. This structured database facilitates streamlined data management and retrieval, supporting various business processes and analyses effectively.
